Core features

Designed for real pets and real humans.

Pawlse focuses on clear signals your pet already gives you: temperature shifts, bursts of activity, and changes in their usual rhythm.

Temperature

See warmth as a pattern.

Track gentle rises and sudden spikes instead of single readings, so you know when the day is just hot—or when it’s worth paying attention.

Activity

Zoomies, naps, and everything between.

Pawlse turns raw motion data into clear “more than usual”, “less than usual”, or “this looks like stress pacing”.

Heart trend

Rhythm over random spikes.

Instead of pretending to decode feelings, Pawlse looks for calm curves, restless jitters, and recovery after play.

Your crew

Switch between pets without losing their story.

Each pet gets its own baselines and history. A sleepy cat should not be judged like a working dog, and Pawlse knows that.

Alert philosophy

Pawlse is built to support, not alarm. It focuses on trends, context, and your pet’s own history.

  • Trend-based, not drama-based
  • Separate rules per pet
  • Band beeps kept gentle and rare

What Makes Pawlse Different

Pawlse doesn’t guess how your pet “feels”. It simply listens to what their body already says and makes it readable.

  • Trend-first: focuses on patterns over time, not single spikes.
  • Species-aware: dog days are not cat days, and Pawlse knows it.
  • Human-friendly: clear language like “chill”, “watch”, or “check now”.
  • Respectful: built to complement, not replace, real veterinary care.

The dream: a simple way to notice changes early, celebrate good days, and understand your pet a little better.
