Why Pawlse?

Built for curious humans and spoiled pets.

Pawlse doesn’t pretend to read minds. It just listens to the quiet signals your pet already sends and turns them into simple, colorful hints you can act on.

  • Trend-first: looks at patterns, not one-off numbers.
  • Species-aware: different baselines for dogs, cats, and tiny chaos bunnies.
  • Human-friendly: words like “chill”, “watch”, and “check now” instead of graphs only.
  • Privacy-light: data stays about play, naps, and walks – not your personal life.

Future versions could sync with vets, export reports, and let you share “best nap of the week” awards with friends.
