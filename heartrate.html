Heart trends, not mind reading.

Pawlse doesn’t try to read emotions. It simply looks at how heart-rate trends move with play, rest, and stress.

Activity vs recovery

PlayCool-downNap

In a real app, you’d see curves showing quick spikes during play and smooth, gradual returns to that pet’s personal “calm” band.

Example insights

  • Strong, clean spikes during ball chasing with no irregular jitters.
  • Smooth cool-down curves after walks, matching previous weeks.
  • Small stress jitters only during heavy storms or loud noises.
